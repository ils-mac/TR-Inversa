# National Institute of Migration

## Applying for a Work Permit in Mexico as a Foreign Temporary Resident

If you are a foreign temporary resident or a temporary student, you can apply for a Work Permit in Mexico.

### Required Documentation

| **Required Document** | **Format** |
|-------------------------|------------------|
| [Format to request immigration stay procedure, filled out electronically through the website, with the autograph signature of the petitioner](https://www.inm.gob.mx/mpublic/publico/inm-tramites.html?a=thgeGuQHx2k%3D&tr=zNxtpmsNikY%3D&h_sdp00=giUnJ9XgtISVcdYJXduIGw%3D%3D&h_dsplp02=en) | Original |
| Valid temporary resident card or temporary resident student card | Original |
| Proof of fee payment under the Federal Act on Fees | Original |
| Job offer letter in letterhead paper from a legally established individual or business, indicating the activity to be performed, the required period of employment, the place of work, and the data of the Employer Registration Certificate duly updated | Original | 

**In case of independent activities, you must attach:**

- [Letter under oath](http://www.inm.gob.mx/static/vun_tramites/Escrito_para_trabajar_de_manera_independiente.pdf) stating the intended occupation and the place where the activities related to such occupation will be carried out. Download and complete. **Original**

- Proof of registration in the Federal Taxpayer Registry. **Copy**

- The temporary resident student, in addition to the resident card, proof of fee payment, and job offer letter, must present a letter of consent from the corresponding educational institution. The above, as long as he/she is pursuing higher education, postgraduate studies and research, and the occupation he/she intends to carry out is related to the subject of his/her studies. **Original**

| **Required Document** | **Format** |
|-------------------------|------------------|
| In the case of persons over fifteen and under eighteen years of age, they must additionally show the permits or authorizations provided for in the labour regulations | Original and copy |

**Note:** Applications must be submitted in original; annexes must be submitted in copy. If the interested party requires an acknowledgement of receipt, he/she must attach a copy for that purpose. (Article 15 A section I of the Administrative Procedure Federal Act)

### Fees

| **Residency type** | **Fee** |
|----------------------|-----------|
| A Temporary resident | $4,001.00 MXN |
| A Temporary resident student | $4,001.00 MXN |

| **Description** | **Amount** |
|--------------|-----------|
| Fee payment for receiving and processing the application and, if applicable, for authorizing the Work Permit | Can vary |

**Note:** To generate the corresponding payment reference number, please access the following [link](http://www.inm.gob.mx/gobmx/word/index.php/pago-de-derechos/).

The payment assistance form must be completed in accordance with the following specifications:

- Verify that the payment corresponds to the type of procedure requested.

- Enter exactly the full given names and last names under which the foreign national is identified in the application, regardless of the number of given names or last names.

- In the case of minors, payment must be registered using the minor’s full given names and last names.

-	It is strongly recommended to thoroughly review the payment receipts issued by banking institutions in order to avoid errors in the applicant’s identification details.

### Options to complete your application

**Online:** Begin your application online, print it, and submit it in person.

**In person:** At the [Institute of Migration Procedures Office](http://www.inm.gob.mx/gobmx/word/index.php/horarios-y-oficinas/) corresponding to the applicant’s place of residence. If the assigned Office of Representation is geographically distant, it is recommended to contact said office by telephone to request information regarding a closer INM office.

**By telephone:** (Information only) 800 00 46264.
